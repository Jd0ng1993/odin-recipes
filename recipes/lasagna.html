LASAGNA

Description

Delicious. Cheesy. Meaty. What else could you want in a dish?!? This lasagne(pronounce LA-SAG-NUG) will absolutely blow your mind. Not only is it THE BEST TASTING LASAGNA EVER but it can also save your marriage, change the oil on your car, wash your laundry, and even walk your dog. That's right no more walking fiddo for you. So what're you waiting for??? Let's do this

  • 1/2 LB Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Jar Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 Can Diced Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Onion Chopped
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Minced
  • 2 Teaspoons Dried Basil
  • 2 Teaspoons Dried Oregano
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 2 Cups Dried Malfada Noodles
  • 1 Cup Shredded Mozarella
    Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Add spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, onion, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat until sauce is hot, about 15 minutes.
  1. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook mafalda noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Add cooked and drained noodles to the sauce and stir until completely coated. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  3. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
  4. Place skillet under the hot broil and cook until cheese is golden and bubbly, 3 to 5 minutes.
